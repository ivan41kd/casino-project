    2. The rules of offering and accepting bets

    The terms used in the rules:

    1. Participant (hereinafter «client») – is a participant in a contract who has entered into betting with the organizer. 
    2. Bet – a bet on a game, the outcome of which depends on the occurrence, non-occurrence, or manner of the event predicted by the client, whereas the event for which the client places the bet is beyond the control of the gambling operator, winning depends on the accuracy of the prediction and the amount of the win depends on the size of the bet and the winning coefficient determined before the bet is made (the terms "toto" and "betting" in the Gambling Act). 
    3. Totalizator – participation in a game where the outcome depends on the result of the selected event. The bet made by the client is beyond the control of the gambling organizer. The winning of a prize depends on whether the bet selection turns out to be correct or not. The amount of the prize depends on the amount of the stake, percentage of the total amount of the bets as determined by the organizer, the number of people who bet correctly and the amount of their bets (named «toto» and «totalizator» in the Gambling Act).
    4. Livebet – a bet made during the course of an event, where the odds will be updated continuously throughout the event according to current state of the event.
    5. Making a bet – a contract concluded between the organizer and the client. In the present case, the content of the contract is the client's betting against the organizer, according to which the losing party must fulfill its obligation to the other party. For the client, this means acknowledging the result of the betting (win or loss) and for the organizer paying out the winnings in accordance with the applicable rules. Betting is concluded upon acceptance of the bet from the client, according to the conditions offered by the organizer.
    6. Stake – an amount of money that is a guarantee for the fulfillment of the client's obligations to the organizer and which is paid by the client to participate in the game by predicting the result of one or a combination of several events.
    7. Outcome – the result of a sporting or other event on which the organizer offered the odds of winning and on which the bet was concluded.
    8. Offers – a list of events and the winning odds of their outcomes which are offered by the organizer for betting.
    9. Events – sports, political and entertainment events taking place in real life on which the organizer is offering the odds of winning.
